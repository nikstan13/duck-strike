## How to Play

1. Open `index.html` in your web browser
2. Click and drag on your players (red team) to aim and shoot
3. Try to score goals in the opponent's net (left side)
4. First to score wins!

## Game Rules

- Cannot score directly from kickoff (foul)
- Ball must completely cross the goal line to score
- Players can enter goals but will be relocated if too far in
- Goal posts provide realistic bounces
